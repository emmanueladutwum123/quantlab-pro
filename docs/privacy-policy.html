QuantLab Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

QuantLab Pro ("the App") is developed and maintained by Emmanuel Adutwum. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. No Data Collection

QuantLab Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All calculations are performed entirely on your device. No account registration is required. No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.

2. No Internet Required

The App works fully offline after installation. It does not make any network requests. No data is sent to any server.

3. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

4. Device Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, etc.).

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
